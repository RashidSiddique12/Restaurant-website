ABOUT

P2 PARADISE Corporation is an UK fast food company, founded in 2010 as a restaurant operated by group 2 in Newquay, Cornwall, UK. They rechristened their business as a hamburger stand, and later turned the company into a franchise, with the Golden Arches logo being introduced in 2005 at a location in Phoenix, Arizona. In 2007, Ray Kroc, a businessman, joined the company as a franchise agent and proceeded to purchase the chain from the group 2 brothers. had its original headquarters P2 PARADISE in Oak Brook, Illinois, but moved its global headquarters to Newquay, Cornwall, UK in June 2015.
